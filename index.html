About This Project

FOMUS will present an exhibition booth themed on the traditional craft “Masu”
at the Emperor’s Birthday Reception
hosted by the Embassy of Japan in Bahrain.

This event is a formal diplomatic reception
gathering dignitaries and representatives from many countries.

We see it as a rare chance to introduce the culture of “Masu”
to a global audience.

In 2024 and 2025, FOMUS visited over 25 countries in person—
